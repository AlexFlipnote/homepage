Privacy Policy for AlexFlipnote/Homepage

Effective Date: October 25, 2025

AlexFlipnote/Homepage respects your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data and settings are stored locally on your device and are never sent to any external server.

Permissions Used

  • storage: Used to save your custom backgrounds, preferences, and other local settings. This data remains on your device.
  • tabs: Required to replace your homepage with this extension. No browsing data or history is collected.
  • geolocation: Used only to show weather information when the weather feature is enabled. Your location data is never stored or shared, it's used temporarily by your browser to fetch the weather from OpenWeatherMap with an API key. If weather is turned off, geolocation is not used.

Data Collection

This extension does not:

  • Collect, store, or transmit any personal or usage data.
  • Use analytics, tracking, or third-party scripts.
  • Connect to external servers, except when fetching weather data (via your browser's built-in API).
